> Nuclear Detonation Detected In Iran
>
> By Dave Franchesca
> The Associated Press
>
> WASHINGTON (Jan. 17) -- Satellites have detected numerous signs of a
> major impact located in the hills of northwestern Iran. "We are pretty
> sure that this was an intended nuclear explosion" Jody Birmingham
> said, a spokeswoman for the Department of Enhanced Satellite
> Technology. When questioned by the press, The White House had no
> comment, nor did The Pentagon. Iranian officials have declined to
> speak with The Associated Press. So far, human casualties or
> structural damage is unknown. More information will be supplied when
> further information is available.
